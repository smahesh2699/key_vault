Understanding Your API Key Token Usage & Costs

✦ What is a Token?

Tokens are raw fragments of text processed by language models. As a general rule, **1 token is about 4 characters of English text**, or roughly **0.75 words**. For reference, a standard paragraph of 100 words consumes approximately 130 to 140 tokens.

✦ Prompt vs. Completion

Model processing cost is divided into:
• **Prompt (Input)**: The context and instructions sent to the model.
• **Completion (Output)**: The words generated by the model in response.
Output tokens are typically priced higher than input tokens by providers.

✦ Cost Estimation Formula

Costs are converted to INR (at ₹83.5/$) based on standard rates per 1,000 tokens:
• **OpenAI**: ~₹0.1670 / 1K (GPT blended rate)
• **Gemini (Google)**: ~₹0.0125 / 1K (Gemini Flash rate)
• **Others**: ~₹0.0835 / 1K (Standard baseline rate)
